2. Description of the Service

IsItHealthy allows users to submit images or descriptions of food items, particularly non-packaged foods. The App utilizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) services (including Google Gemini), is based on general principles, and uses our own algorithms to provide an estimated informational score (0-100) and related guidance regarding the potential healthfulness of the food item.

This score is generated based on algorithms informed by publicly available principles from recognized health organizations and frameworks, such as the World Health Organization (WHO), the NOVA food classification system, and balanced diet models like the Harvard Healthy Eating Plate.

Important: The score and any analysis provided are intended for general informational and educational purposes only. Due to significant variations in non-packaged food ingredients, preparation, and portion sizes, the score is an estimate, not a precise nutritional measurement. Images processed by AI are handled as described in our Privacy Policy.
